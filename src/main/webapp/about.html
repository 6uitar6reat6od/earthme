About the cool guys who did this...


We're a group of five computer engineer students from Zaragoza.


Boosted by @borjaeg and our great leader teacher @fjlopezpellicer we presented a complete different project,

but due to SpaceApps 2K14 restrictions on challenges, it was turned down...(maybe in the future...)


EarthMe! aims to compose people's photos with satellite images, all about our wonderful blue planet in order

to aware them on the variety of colours the Earth gathers beneath their feet, and wraps their environment.


Yeah, Earth on Earth hardcore recursion
